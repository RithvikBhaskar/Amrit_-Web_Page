The expansive and serene Brahmasthanam Temple Complex at Edappally, Kochi, by the NH-17 is home to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Kochi, which is candidly achieving fame and glory. The University founded by the well-known humanitarian, Sadguru Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, offers academic education rooted in Indian cultural history. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-campus, multi-disciplinary research university that is rated as one of the top research universities in India and is certified by NAAC with a “A++” rating. The Kochi campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham houses schools of Business, Medicine, Dentistry, Nursing, Pharmacy, Nano Sciences, and Allied Health Sciences in proximity. A massive, ancient Banyan tree dominates the scene, creating the ideal environment and presenting the traditional knowledge of India. Modern amenities including laboratories, a large auditorium, a well-stocked library, well-connected smart-classrooms, conference rooms, seminar halls, and more, are artfully juxtaposed. Like how the college canteen nurtures the body while the temple nestled away on campus offers spiritual comfort. The campus library is creatively charged with numerous literary works, recollections, memoirs, and essays by award-winning authors, journalists, and academicians on Literature, Computer Applications & IT, Media, Social Sciences, Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Cultural Studies, Commerce and Management, Business Administration, and more making the rounds. A sizable, well-maintained farmland extends in the expanse of the campus where organic farming is practiced, resulting in a beautiful ambience that stimulates the body, mind, and spirit. Courses offered at Amrita Vishwa Vidhyapeetam, Amritapuri campus are:

  • Engineering
  • Business
  • Physical Sciences
